Grilled Cheese

Discription

The Grilled Cheese is a classic sandwich adored around the world.

It is sandwich that is filled with cheese then toasted to get the outside crunchy and the inside gooye

Ingredients

  • Bread Slices
  • Your Choice of cheese
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Butter Your bread slices on both side (make sure you coat every inch)
  2. Add your cheese choice in between two slices of bread
  3. Get a pan and put it on medium heat
  4. Add a table spoon of Olive oil into the pan
  5. Place your bread in the pan
  6. Cook each side for about 3-minutes

There you go you have made the most simple and most perfect Grilled Cheese Sandwich.
